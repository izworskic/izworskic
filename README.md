# Chris Izworski

**Writer · Gardener · Technologist** — Bay City, Michigan

Solutions Consultant at [Prepared911](https://www.prepared911.com). Former 911 Executive Director (Saginaw County) and 911 Director (Bay County). 20+ years in emergency services, public safety technology, and organizational leadership. Certified Public Manager. Northwood University BBA, Magna Cum Laude.
